This dataset contains information about the performance of students in various subjects. Each row represents a student's performance, and the columns represent different attributes such as gender, race/ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch type, test preparation course, and scores in math, reading, and writing.
gender: Gender of the student (male/female)
race/ethnicity: Race or ethnicity group of the student
parental level of education: Educational level of the student's parents
lunch: Type of lunch received by the student (standard/free/reduced)
test preparation course: Whether the student completed a test preparation course (none/completed)
math score: Score obtained by the student in math
reading score: Score obtained by the student in reading
writing score: Score obtained by the student in writing
